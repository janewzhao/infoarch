Transcripts

Full Academic Transcripts

The full academic transcript is an official University document that lists all the module marks and grades you have achieved on your course.

Eligibility

As a taught campus-based student you will automatically receive one full academic transcript upon successful completion of your course. However, please note that full academic transcripts are currently not available to students of the following:

  • courses in the School of Medicine
  • distance learning courses
  • taught postgraduate courses completed prior to 2009
  • Education, PGcert and PGCE courses
  • Lifelong Learning courses

If you have recently graduated in one of the above courses please contact your department for further guidance on what documentation is available to you.
