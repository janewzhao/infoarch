Alumni

The Alumni Office exists to maintain strong links with graduates, parents, friends and supporters of the University through a programme of events, services and communications. We are also responsible for encouraging and stewarding philanthropic gifts to support a range of funding needs including bursaries for students, campus facilities and state-of-the-art research equipment. We are always eager to enhance the range of benefits we can offer - please contact us if you have any suggestions.
